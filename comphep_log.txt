Plan og logbog over udskejelser i CompHEP.

X = Færdiggjort
/ = Igangværende

Vi har redigeret model-filen så at EE = GG = 1.
De oprindelige værdier er; EE = 0.31345, GG = 1.21358.

The following data sets are made with s-channel virtual gluons.
Beam energy of 7500 GeV.
Elementary charge EE and strong coupling constant GG both equal to 1.
Cuts are T3 min = 50, T4 min = 50 and no max.
Itmax = 1 (to avoid optimization on negative values)
Number of bins = 300.

Alle data-filer findes i rå tilstand i /trunk/data/ og samlede (i gnuplot-venligt format, eller endet) i /trunk/data/analyse/.
